1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
The richest mutation class and largely well-built. The of(...) constructor-mirroring
family, the entity-mapping create(...) family (single/collection x naming-policy x
selected-props), addColumn overloads (String/Object, byte[]/byte[], ByteBuffer, with/
without timestamp), add(Cell), and the toPut(...) converters are coherent, and the
cell-overwrite-vs-versioning semantics are documented thoroughly. The commented-out
deprecated addImmutable block is correctly left out of the API. The only notable issue
is a cross-class consistency gap: AnyPut (like AnyDelete) lacks the setAttribute(String,
byte[]) overload that AnyAppend/AnyIncrement add — see the recommendation below.

None.
