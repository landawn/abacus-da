1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
A clean, thin standalone adapter over org.apache.hadoop.hbase.client.RowMutations.
Factory set (of(Object), of(Object,int), of(byte[]), of(byte[],int)), val(), the two add
overloads, and getRow/equals/hashCode/toString are correct and well documented (including
the surprising HBase semantics that equals/compareTo consider the row key only). Two
consistency/coverage gaps stand out versus the rest of the package.

None.

None.

None.

5. MISSING METHODS
[1] getMutations() / size() / isEmpty() pass-throughs | AnyRowMutations.java:231-322 | Low |
    Callers cannot inspect the batch without reaching through val(). Every AnyMutation
    subclass exposes size()/isEmpty(), so the absence here is a parity gap. Recommendation:
    add getMutations() (and/or size()/isEmpty()). Confirming check: verify
    RowMutations.getMutations()/getMaxPriority() exist in the targeted HBase version before
    mirroring.

6. DEPRECATION / REMOVAL CANDIDATES
[1] compareTo(Row) | AnyRowMutations.java:369 (@SuppressWarnings("deprecation")@368, no
    @Deprecated) | Med | Delegates to the same deprecated HBase Row.compareTo as
    AnyMutation.compareTo, which IS marked @Deprecated (AnyMutation.java:674-677). VERIFIED.
