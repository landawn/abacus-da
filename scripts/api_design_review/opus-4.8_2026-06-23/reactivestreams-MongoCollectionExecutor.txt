1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
A faithful reactive mirror of the blocking mongodb/MongoCollectionExecutor: list->Flux, query->Mono<DataSet>,
findFirst->Mono, and all write/find-and-modify/distinct/aggregate/groupBy/watch families are 1:1 with the
blocking class, parameter ordering matches exactly, and insertOne/insertMany correctly return
Mono<InsertOneResult/InsertManyResult> (an improvement over the blocking void). The only concrete defect is
an accessor name drift; the other deltas are by-design consequences of Mono lacking a present-but-null state.
