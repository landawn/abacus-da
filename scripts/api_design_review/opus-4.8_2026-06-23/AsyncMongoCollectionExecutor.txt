1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
A faithful async twin of MongoCollectionExecutor: every method delegates the blocking call to a backing
AsyncExecutor and returns ContinuableFuture<T> (ContinuableFuture<Void> for void ops), correctly keeping
Optional/Nullable/OptionalXxx payloads INSIDE the future rather than collapsing to null. No per-method
Executor param (held at construction). No mis-typed or mis-ordered wrappers. The only issues are missing
wrappers (sync ops not mirrored here) and a stability-annotation mismatch.
