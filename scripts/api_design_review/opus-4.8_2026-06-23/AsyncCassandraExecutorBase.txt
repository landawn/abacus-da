1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
A clean, complete 1:1 async mirror of CassandraExecutorBase: identical names, overload shapes, parameter
order, and generics, with ContinuableFuture-wrapped returns that preserve the Optional/Nullable/null(gett)
payload distinctions. The threading model uses the native driver executeAsync (no per-call Executor param)
— correct, and intentionally simpler than HBase's AsyncExecutor model. The only substantive issue is a
missing stability annotation across a large family.

None.
